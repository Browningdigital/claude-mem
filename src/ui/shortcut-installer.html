Save to Browning

Send any URL, file, photo, or PDF from your iPhone directly to the Browning Digital content pipeline. Everything is extracted, processed, and stored automatically.

One tap to install. Handles links, PDFs, photos, and files.
Works from Safari, Claude, Twitter, Instagram — any app.

After Installing

1

Tap "Add Shortcut"

When the Shortcuts app opens, tap Add Shortcut to confirm the install.

2

Share from any app

Open Safari, Twitter, Instagram, Claude, or any app. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).

3

Tap "Save to Browning"

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap Save to Browning. You'll see a notification when it's saved.

Links are extracted and processed (articles, social posts, YouTube transcripts).
Files (PDFs, photos, documents) are uploaded directly to the pipeline.

Manual Setup (if download doesn't work)

▶
Only use these instructions if the one-tap install above doesn't work on your device. The shortcut file handles everything automatically.
1

Create a new shortcut

Open the Shortcuts app. Tap + in the top right. Name it Save to Browning.

2

Enable sharing

Tap the ℹ icon, toggle on Show in Share Sheet. Under share types, select anything.

3

Add "Get URLs from Input"

Search for Get URLs from and add it. Input: Shortcut Input.

4

Add If block

Add If action. Condition: URLs (from step 3) has any value.

5

If (links): Get Contents of URL

URL: https://content-extractor.devin-b58.workers.dev/api/share?url= + URLs variable.

6

Otherwise (files): POST upload

URL: https://content-extractor.devin-b58.workers.dev/api/upload
Method: POST, Body: Form, File field: key=file, value=Shortcut Input.

7

After End If: Show Notification

Message: Saved to Browning!
