Manual Setup (if link above doesn't work)

Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone. Create a new shortcut named "Save to Browning". Set it to Show in Share Sheet and receive URLs.

Add a single action: Get Contents of URL with:

https://content-extractor.devin-b58.workers.dev/api/share?url=[Share Sheet Input]

Then add Show Notification: "Saved to Browning!"
